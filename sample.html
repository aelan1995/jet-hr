BUSINESS MODEL AND SOURCE OF PROFITS

Coffee Core aims to produce dry extracts rich in polyphenols and/or melanoidins with the aim of selling them to small, medium and large cosmetic companies. Coffee Core wants to reach them through direct marketing solutions and participation in specific sector fairs, such as cosmetics, food and chemicals.

COMPETITORS

Competitors can be divided into direct and indirect. The first are two companies located in Denmark and Canada, which produce bioextracts from spent coffee grounds. Coffee Core differs from them thanks to an innovative extraction method based on natural solvents. Indirect competitors, on the other hand, start directly from green coffee, therefore as an alternative to the food supply chain and with significantly higher raw material costs.

Short description of the aspects of innovation that characterize the business enterprise

Coffee Core uses waste material as the primary source for the production of biophenols, giving new life to coffee grounds that would otherwise be thrown away. Furthermore, Coffee Core uses a totally innovative extraction method because it is based on natural solvents which reduce the carbon footprint of the finished product and which lower production costs, compared to the use of classic extraction methods which are much more energy intensive.
